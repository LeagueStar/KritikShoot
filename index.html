HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Survive the waves. Kill enemies to earn XP and level up, dodge the bosses that show up every few waves, and grab dropped coins — they carry over between runs and can be spent in the shop.

Desktop Controls

  • WASD — Move
  • Mouse — Aim
  • Arrows — Aim (keyboard-only players; overrides mouse while held)
  • Click (hold to keep firing) — Shoot
  • Space — Shoot (toward aim, or nearest enemy)
  • E / Shift — Switch weapon
  • Esc — Pause
  • Q — Quit to menu (while paused)

Mobile Controls

  • Joystick (left side) — Move
  • Drag (right side) — Aim
  • FIRE button — Shoot
  • Weapon button — Cycle weapon
  • ⏸ — Pause
  • ⌂ — Quit to menu (while paused)

Weapons

Cycle through three weapons: GUN → SHOTGUN → LASER, each with its own spread, damage, and fire rate.

Upgrades

Leveling up lets you pick one upgrade: Move Speed, Max Health, Damage, Fire Rate, Bullet Speed, Crit Chance, or Lifesteal. Coins collected in a run also unlock permanent upgrades in the persistent shop between runs.
